Our Story

SlangField Landscape Services was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality land development services to farmers, property owners, and construction companies in Botswana.

What started as a small fencing company has grown into a comprehensive land development enterprise, offering services ranging from borehole drilling to heavy machinery rental. Our growth is a testament to our commitment to quality work and customer satisfaction.

Today, we employ over 50 skilled professionals and have completed more than 500 projects across the country. We continue to invest in modern equipment and training to stay at the forefront of the industry.

🎯

Our Story

Building Botswana's landscape, one project at a time.

Slangfield Landscape Services was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional, and affordable land development services to farms, plots, and commercial properties across Botswana.

With over 10 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for quality workmanship and exceptional customer service. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering results that exceed expectations.

From fencing installation to borehole drilling, we have successfully completed hundreds of projects, helping our clients transform their land and achieve their goals.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, reliable, and affordable landscape services that meet the unique needs of each client. We are committed to excellence in every project, big or small.

Our Vision

To be the leading landscape services company in Botswana, known for our professionalism, innovation, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

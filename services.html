Farm & Plot Fencing

Professional fencing solutions for agricultural lands, farms, and plots. We install durable wooden, metal, and wire fencing that protects your property and livestock for years to come.

What We Offer:

  • Wooden Fencing - Traditional and durable wooden fences perfect for farm boundaries and livestock enclosures.
  • Metal Fencing - Long-lasting metal fences including chain-link, welded mesh, and steel panels.
  • Electric Fencing - Modern electric fence systems for enhanced security and livestock management.
  • Gate Installation - Custom gates designed to match your fencing, including automated options.

Why Choose Us:

  • Quality materials sourced from trusted suppliers
  • Experienced installation teams
  • Competitive pricing with no hidden costs

Borehole Drilling

Expert borehole drilling services to access clean underground water. We use advanced drilling technology for efficient and reliable water solutions that serve your needs for decades.

What We Offer:

  • Site Survey - Hydrogeological survey to determine the best drilling location and expected water yield.
  • Deep Drilling - Professional drilling using modern equipment to reach aquifers at various depths.
  • Pump Installation - Installation of submersible pumps, hand pumps, or solar-powered pumping systems.
  • Water Testing - Laboratory testing to ensure water quality meets safety standards.

Why Choose Us:

  • State-of-the-art drilling equipment
  • Experienced hydrogeologists on staff
  • Complete turnkey solutions
  • After-sales support and maintenance
